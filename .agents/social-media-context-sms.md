## Anti-Patterns

- **Topics off-limits**: boudoir / undress (charged-but-clothed only), diet/weight-loss culture, anything requiring an altered (tanned/warmed) aesthetic, impersonating or drifting toward any real person.
- **Tones to avoid**: hype-y, preachy, motivational-poster, try-hard, fake-deep.
- **Content types to avoid**: anime (separate audience — excluded), car-spec/performance talk (wrong audience), undisclosed ads.
- **Non-negotiable**: native AI-content label on every export; ad disclosure (#ad / "Paid partnership") prominent in the caption body, never buried in a hashtag stack (ACL/AANA — bible Part 7).
