04

Construction & finishing

  • Front structure. Canvassed front with hair-canvas chest piece for a clean architectural lapel roll; fusible support elsewhere. Roll line pressed and set.
  • Closure. Double-breasted 6×2 stance, front overlap 16 cm, internal jigger button. Buttonholes: keyhole, confirm hand vs machine at sample.
  • Lapel & collar. Peak lapel ≈11 cm at peak; undercollar melton, felled. Hard press, no shine — dry charcoal must stay matte.
  • Waist. Cinched via attached self-belt on belt loops (front); back half-belt with 2 buttons. Confirm loop vs. keeper placement at proto.
  • Sleeves. Set-in, roll-head with sleeve-head roll + light shoulder pad; 4 cuff buttons each (working vs. non-working — advise cost delta).
  • Pockets. Two besom/welt at side seam + one interior welt with button.
  • Vent & hems. Centre-back vent 30 cm, clean-finished. Hem 4 cm, blind-stitched; lining dropped with a pleat.
  • Seams & edges. Seams bound/overlocked; front edge clean or 6 mm edge-stitch — send both on the proto so we can choose.
05

Labelling & packing

  • Brand label woven, centre-back neck. Care/content + fibre + RN/CoO on side-seam label; size tab beneath.
  • Content/care bilingual per destination market; symbols per ISO 3758. Country of origin per your facility.
  • Pack: on a wooden/loop hanger in a breathable garment bag; optional fold-pack per instruction; polybag food-grade if used.
06

To quote — what we need from you

This is a first development sheet. Please make one fit proto, size M, charcoal, and quote against a made-to-order model.

  • Sample cost + lead time for the size-M proto.
  • Unit cost at MOQ 10 / 30 / 50, and a per-order (made-to-order) unit cost + turnaround — this is our primary model.
  • Fabric: can you source the coating wool + linings, or should we supply? Send your coating books / handloom options.
  • Grading: confirm the graded nest and the POM tolerances above; flag anything that risks the sculptural line.
  • Value engineering: call out any construction change that protects the look while improving cost or lead time.
